Muhammad Ali Malik

An accomplished academician and media professional. A gold medalist, topped both his bachelor's and master's programs in Media Studies. He has served as a permanent faculty member at Riphah International University and Foundation University Islamabad, and as a visiting lecturer at TMUC Pakistan. His creative work in media has been recognized with two National Awards for musical films in 2021. He is currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Creative Multimedia at Multimedia University, Malaysia, on scholarship with a Graduate Research Assistant position.
